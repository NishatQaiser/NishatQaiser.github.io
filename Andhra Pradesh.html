HYDERABAD TOURISM

Hyderabad, the City of Pearls, is the capital of Telangana and Andhra Pradesh and named after Hyder Mahal, wife of the ruler Qutab Shah. More than 400 years old Hyderabad today is the fifth largest metropolis of India and is known for its history, culture, heritage monuments crafts Charminar, Golconda fort, Hussain Sagar Lake and Srisailam, are some of the tourist places you could visit here
